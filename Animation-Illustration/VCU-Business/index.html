As the Digital Marketing Intern for the Communications and Marketing Department at the VCU School of Business, I provided design expertise to each of the school's departments. I assisted with the creation and management of social media content, website design and content updates, and the development of digital and printed media for student resources and events.
  My most impactful contribution during my time in this role was the creation and implementation of a number of graphic digital assets used to improve the school's web presence. This included unifying the school's social media with a new, cohesive graphics for profile photos, banner images, and highlight covers; new website icons, both universal as well as department-specific; an informative and versatile motion graphic used to inform our audience of the termination of unused social media accounts; and a new take on the new-student orientation video for the Money Spot, a free financial counseling service available to students.
