TITLE: Made For Walkin'
PROJECT: Animation, Photography, Web Development
YEAR: 2019

Made for Walkin' is a personal tribute to the pair of shoes that carried me for over seven years of my adolescence. I chose to animate photography of the shoes themselves to maintain a sense of authenticity, and combined that with hand-drawn animation of landmarks from my hometown.
  Each frame of this video was individually shot, drawn, and edited. This is an interactive project. Try putting your own spin on the animation below or click here to open in a new tab!
