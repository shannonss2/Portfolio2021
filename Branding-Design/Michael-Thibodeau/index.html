INFORMATION

CLIENT: Michael Thibodeau
SERVICES: Logo Design, Brand Photography
YEAR: 2020

Michael Thibodeau is a Richmond-based creative artist. After seven years of working as an actor in the Orlando Amusement Park scene, he moved back to his hometown of Richmond, VA and transitioned to writing and directing for short films and music videos. His work perfectly combines retro influences and aesthetics with purely modern stories, and tends to explore themes of human connection, self-acceptance, and home. 
  His logo treatment is based on a traditional-style font, but with a contemporary playfulness.
