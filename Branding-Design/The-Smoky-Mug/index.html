INFORMATION

CLIENT: Smoky Mug
SERVICES: Menu Design, Brand Development
YEAR: 2021

The Smoky Mug is a local coffee shop that opened in the Northside neighborhood of Richmond in the fall of 2020.
  I assisted the owners with a few projects to further develop their brand as they were establishing themselves in the community. I designed all of their menus, including a printed flat and brochure, as well as hand writing their indoor hanging menu board. I also put the finishing touches on their facade by hand-painting the logo and building number.
