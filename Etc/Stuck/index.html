TITLE: Stuck
PROJECT: Photography, Photo Editing
YEAR: 2020

During quarantine many artists began to feel stuck, both physically and creatively, without the support or opportunities which normally help to power our artistic endeavors. We captured these feelings by using surrealism within the confines of our homes.
A collaboration with Michael Thibodeau.

Featured in Gallery5's A Year Apart exhibition.
